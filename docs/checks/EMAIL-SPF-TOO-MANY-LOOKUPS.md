**SPF evaluation stays within the 10-lookup limit**

## Description

RFC 7208 §4.6.4 caps SPF DNS lookups at 10 per evaluation. Exceeding it causes a PermError — receivers typically treat this as a fail, allowing spoofed mail to pass.
